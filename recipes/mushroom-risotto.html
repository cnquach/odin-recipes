Mushroom Risotto Recipe

Description

This is a very tasty recipe for mushroom risotto.

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice
  • White wine
  • Vegetable stock
  • Mushrooms
  • Shallot
  • Parmagiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. Gently fry shallots
  2. Add rice and stir and cook until translucent
  3. Add white wine and boil away, keep stirring
